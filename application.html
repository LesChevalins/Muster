WHAT IS PISTACHE?

Pistache is a free app that helps children aged 6 to 11 to complete their daily missions.

WHY USING PISTACHE ?

A benevolent accomplice which contributes to your child's development.

A playful and intuitive solution to manage your children's missions like you want it.

Develop your child's skills and knowledge through modern teaching and games.

Trust your children with responsibilities starting today and help them prepare for their adult life.

Create and assign missions to your children.

Have fun completing your mission.

Access tons of games and hand-picked videos.

Validate your mission to let your parents know you're done.

Choose within over 25 preconfigured missions.

Or create your own!

Discover the best educational apps…

And assign them as missions for your children!

Congratulate your child by offering him access to tons of games and hand-picked videos!
