The three of us wanted to create something original and different in a domain we love: education. Today, most educational apps concentrate on classical learning like maths or foreign languages, and that’s great ! Still, we thought new technologies could also allow kids to learn things that are not taught in school.

Parents themselves gave us the idea to help kids in their daily missions. We also discovered research that showed the link between accomplishing daily chores at a young age and professional and personal success once you’re all grown up.

This is how Pistache was born!
