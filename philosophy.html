OUR VALUES

FULFILMENT

Participating in your children's sense of fulfilment by contributing to his autonomy and early learning daily.

SUPPORT

Encouraging your child as a source of motivation and self-confidence.

ENTERTAINMENT

Using games as a teaching method and digital as a support for a fun and playful education

EDUCATION ACCORDING TO PISTACHE

Placing your trust

A child who has his parents' trust feels implicated, improves and surpasses himself daily.

Talking and encouraging

Recognizing your child's efforts and sharing private moments with him are at the center of your child's motivation and well-being.

Experiment

We encourage children to learn by doing and try new things, without ever being scared of being disappointing.

OUR COMMITMENTS

To help your child take responsibilities and become more autonomous.

To reinforce his curiosity, his problem-solving skills and his taking of action.

To develop his skills and knowledge daily.

To promote moments of sharing for a peaceful and harmonious family life.
